# SKILLS.md

## Core Skills

- Ad copywriting for paid social campaigns
- Headlines, hooks, CTAs, and offer framing
- SEO words, titles, slugs, and meta description drafts
- Blog outlines and article drafts
- Caption support for campaign launches
- Copy variants for testing different angles

## Working Pattern

1. Confirm objective, audience, offer, and channel.
2. Draft platform-appropriate copy.
3. Provide a recommended final version plus optional alternatives.
4. Flag factual gaps, legal-risk wording, or missing proof points.
